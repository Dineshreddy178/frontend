Grammar Courses

Grammar is unfortunately ignored in most schools’ curricula. Our grammar courses are designed to help students improve their communication skills, reading comprehension skills and build a sense of logic.

Writing Courses

Writing is one skill everyone needs, no matter what path they follow! Our writing courses provide students with the skills to write essays for high school and university applications, humanities and arts courses, and standardized tests like the SSAT and SAT.

Latin Courses

Latin provides learners of all ages with an abstract understanding of grammar, cognate words that can help standardized test scores, and the skills to master other languages. On top of this, students will learn about how Roman history and culture helped to shape the world today.!

Why Choose Us

Development of Strong Critical Thinking Skills

Our students don’t just memorize facts, they learn how to evaluate information, identify logical fallacies, and construct persuasive arguments. This translates to better decision-making, problem-solving, and the ability to discern truth in a world flooded with information.

Preparation for Lifelong Learning

The traditional school model often compartmentalizes subjects. Logos Academy’s emphasis on interconnectedness helps students see the big picture. Students will develop a natural curiosity and love for learning that lasts a lifetime, making them adaptable in a world that demands constant upskilling.

Nurturing of Compassionate Leaders

The Liberal Arts foster an appreciation for diverse cultures and ideas, encouraging empathy and ethical decision-making. Our students will not only be prepared to excel academically but also to lead with integrity and contribute positively to society..

Personalized Attention and Mentorship

At Logos Academy, we ensure that every student receives personalized guidance and support through prompt feedback on assignments, as well as mentorship during class. We are keen to foster each student’s unique strengths and talents.
